How It Works

Order your favorite food in 3 simple steps

1. Browse Restaurants

Explore local restaurants, view menus, and find your favorite dishes. Filter by cuisine, rating, or delivery time.

2. Place Your Order

Add items to cart, choose delivery or pickup, select payment method (Telesom, E-Dahab, or Cash), and confirm your order.

3. Fast Delivery

A nearby rider picks up your order and delivers it fast. Track your delivery in real-time on the map.
